Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide awareness to would-be customers about the existence of the services provided by Whitewater Rafting, as well as to increase revenue brought about by the influx of customers who came from the website.

Audience

People ranging in ages from youth to working age adults who are looking for something thrilling to do during their stay in the area. These people would also be ones who have access to any device capable of connecting to the internet.
